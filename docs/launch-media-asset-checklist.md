## 3. Upload Guidelines & Image Preparation Tips

### Practical Image Preparation Tips
- **Format**: WEBP or JPG recommended.
- **File Size**: Keep images under 1.5MB when possible. System max image size is 5MB.
- **Composition**: Do not place important text inside images. Keep the subject near the center so cropping works well on both desktop and mobile.
- **Quality**: Avoid watermark, unclear license, and cheap stock-looking images.

### Alt Text Guidelines
Alt text should be descriptive but not keyword-stuffed. It must describe the image for accessibility and SEO.
- **Good**: "VAVAW cinematic beauty atmosphere", "VAVAW Cosmetic clean beauty editorial", "Soft cosmetic texture and ritual detail"
- **Bad**: "makeup", "cosmetics buy online", "image123"

### Upload Location
- Navigate to **Admin Dashboard -> Media**.
- Select the appropriate `site_key` (`main` or `cosmetic`).
- Set type to `image`.
